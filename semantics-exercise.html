The Life of Aardvarks

Eating Habits

They use their noses to eat. They eat ants and termites most of the time ,but occasionally they eat beetle larvae.

Movement

They can dig really fast and they are good at eluding predators, moving in a zig-zag fashion and flipping themselves on their back to kick at predators.

Sleeping Habits

They are nocturnal animals. They come out at night to feed and burrow to make holes to escape the heat during the day.
